About ValorTrust

ValorTrust Integrated Services Ltd is a CAC-registered Nigerian company built on the conviction that structured, professional, multi-sector services can drive real and lasting value for businesses, communities, and partners across Nigeria and Africa.

Our Story

Founded in Kano, Nigeria in July 2023 and formally registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in February 2026 under RC Number 9268182, ValorTrust Integrated Services Ltd has been committed to building a business model that is not only scalable but structured well enough to inspire confidence from clients, investors, and government bodies alike.

Operating through twelve (12) specialised sectors with clearly defined roles, responsibilities, and governance structures, ValorTrust delivers consistent quality, accountability, and professional excellence across construction, agriculture, technology, energy, trade, and several other high-impact industries.
